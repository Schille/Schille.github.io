About Michael

Hello there, I am Michael - a business director and software engineer. I have a passion for technology and for great teamwork. As a tech leader, I strive to inspire and motivate my teams, fostering an environment of collaboration, innovation, psychological safety, and continuous improvement. I value excellence and attention to detail in order to consistently deliver top-notch software products and services.

You can find me at conferences where I speak about tech and startup topics. Besides my work, I love to spend time with my family. I like to craft beer, do all kinds of sports, go camping, play the keyboard and ukulele and develop new ideas. I post my opinions on LinkedIn.
